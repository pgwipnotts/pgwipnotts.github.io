POSTGRADUATE
WOMEN IN PHYSICS
CONFERENCE

Friday 6th March 2020 at The University of Nottingham

The Postgraduate Women in Physics Conference will be a one day conference all about the careers of Women in Physics after a PhD. The aim of the day is to inspire fellow students and highlight potential avenues for a career post-PhD. We are are hoping to have 5-6 speakers from both academia and industry at the event to talk about their career paths and their experiences of being a woman in physics. There will also be a poster competition. It should be a great way for students to come together, network, and find out about what is possible after completing a PhD in physics.

Organising Committee:

This conference is being organised by a team of postgraduate and undergraduate students from the School of Physics and Astronomy at University of Nottingham. Please feel free to contact us with any questions about the day.
